Privacy Policy

Effective: January 2026

Perpetual Genesis is a privacy-first planning app. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store personal information.

Data storage

All plans, timelines, and settings are stored locally on your device (for example, in your browser’s local storage). This data is not sent to our servers.

Backups and exports (user-initiated)

If you choose to export or create a backup (available in Pro store versions), the exported file is saved by you to a location you choose. Perpetual Genesis does not automatically upload backups and does not receive a copy of your exported data.

Third-party services

Perpetual Genesis does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Children’s privacy

Perpetual Genesis is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information.
