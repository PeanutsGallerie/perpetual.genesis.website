Privacy Policy

Effective: January 2026

Perpetual Genesis is a privacy-first planning app. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store personal data.

Data storage

All app data remains on your device (for example, in your browser’s local storage). Nothing is sent to our servers.

Backups and exports

If you choose to export or create a backup (Pro store versions), the exported file is saved by you to a location you choose. Perpetual Genesis does not automatically upload backups and does not receive a copy of your exported data.

Third-party services

No accounts. No analytics. No ads. No tracking. No third-party services used to collect user data.
